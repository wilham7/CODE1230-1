The Australian Bureau of Statistics collects data about the population every five years in the Census. This data is available to the public and can be analysed to understand the demographic and spread of the Australian population. The Dataset G06 is a collection of the marital status of the Australian population. The data is divided into 3 variables: Marital Status, Gender and Age.
